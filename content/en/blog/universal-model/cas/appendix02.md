# Appendix 2: 
## The MDV Hardware Sensors<br />(Mass, Density, Variance)

These pillars are categorized as **State Variables**. They represent the physical and human "hardware" that determines a system's capacity to perform work and its durability when subjected to shocks.

### 1. Systemic Mass (): Inertia & Capacitance

Mass represents the "Buffer" or "Thermal Mass" of the society. High scores indicate a system with significant stored potential that can resist sudden changes in velocity (shocks).
